Privacy Policy

Katha Cards · Last updated 18 June 2026

Katha Cards is a trivia app about the epics and history of India. This policy explains what information the app handles and why. We collect as little as possible and do not sell your data or use third-party advertising or tracking.

What we collect

  • Game progress & settings. Your scores, which cards you've seen, and your free-round count are stored on your device (browser local storage). They stay on your device unless you sign in to an optional account to sync them.
  • A random device identifier. A randomly generated ID is stored on your device to remember that you've unlocked Premium. It is not linked to your name or any advertising ID.
  • Email address (optional). If you sign in or restore a purchase by email, we use your email to identify your subscription. We never use it for marketing without consent.
  • Purchase information. Subscriptions are processed by Apple (App Store), Google (Google Play), or Stripe (web). We receive your subscription status; we never receive or store your full payment-card details.

How your information is used

  • To unlock and verify your Premium subscription.
  • To save and (optionally) sync your game progress.
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.

Who we share it with

We share information only with the service providers needed to run the app: our payment processors (Apple, Google, and/or Stripe) and, if you use an account, our authentication and database provider (Supabase). Each processes data under its own privacy policy. We do not sell personal information.

Data retention & your choices

Progress kept on your device can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data. If you have an account, you can request access to or deletion of your data, including account deletion, by emailing us. We will delete your account data within a reasonable period of such a request.

Children

Katha Cards is a general-audience, educational app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
